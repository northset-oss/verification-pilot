# Northset OSS Run Records — Maintainer Respect Policy

Northset OSS contribution work follows each repository's current public contribution rules.
Maintainers control their project and owe Northset no review, explanation, publication, product
participation, or promotional reference.

## Boundaries

- Keep changes small, issue-linked, and narrowly disclosed.
- Do not post promotional content, run invitations, acquisition links, or separate receipt
  comments in upstream repositories.
- Do not treat a public issue, pull request, label, review, CI run, merge, closure, or silence as
  consent for a different purpose.
- Do not contest a rejection or criticize maintainers.
- Stop and withdraw public projection authority when consent is withdrawn.

## Independent consent

Contribution invitation, verification execution, receipt publication, and marketing reference
are four independent scopes. Every granted scope requires its own retained evidence. Missing
receipt-publication consent suppresses the named public receipt. Missing marketing-reference
consent prohibits product demonstrations, promotional copy, repository aggregates, testimonials,
and acquisition material even when technical receipt publication is allowed.

A correction may preserve a sparse incident record at an unlisted direct URL. It must not
restore the prior technical projection or imply endorsement.

## Claims

A run record reports the exact declared execution evidence in its immutable bundle. It is not a
security review, code-quality judgment, maintainer verification, approval, merge recommendation,
or product recommendation. Attestation records artifact provenance only.

No payment is tied to merge or approval. See the [Payment Policy](payment_policy.md).
